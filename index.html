The Beginning of My Journey

As a child, I was enchanted by colors, shapes, and stories. My mother, an artist, filled our home with creativity, inspiring me to dream of being an artist or photographer. Though my path led to robotics, these passions shaped who I am. This page is a love letter to those early dreams, a journey through the art and moments that define me.
